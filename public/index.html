About Elite Cuts

A cut above the rest, since day one.

Founded in 2010 by master barber Marcus Reed, Elite Cuts has grown from a single chair to the city's most sought-after barbershop. We blend old-world technique — straight razors, hot towels, precision shears — with a modern, members-club atmosphere.
